Zekr Glossary
This is a small glossary for the meaning of some quranic terms.

Quran
An Arabic book sent down continuously to the last prophet - Muhammad (pbuh) - by Allah during a period of 23 years. It is also the miracles of The last prophet. The Quran is divided into 30 Juzs, and 114 Suras. Every sura has a number of sub parts called Aya. There may be more or less than a sura in a juz an there is a number of ayas, which introduce a sura.
Sura
Also may be written as Surah, or Sura. There are 114 different chapters in the Quran, each of them is called a Sura. A sura may have one or more subjects within it. Suras are of two type of Makki and Madani.
Aya
The smallest part of a sura, which consits of one or more sentences, or even a single word. There is a sura with 286 ayas (Al-Baqarah) and another sura (Al-Kawsar) with 3 Ayas.
Juz
An abstract division of The Quran which divides it into 30 nearly equal parts.
Makki
Suras sent down to the prophet in Mecca are called Makki (which means related to Mecca).
Madani
Suras sent down to the prophet in Medina are called Madani (which means related to Medina).
Miracle of the Quran
Quran, is the only sacred writing, which claims to be a miracle. It asserts that no one can make something like a Quran sura at all.
